VersionTracker

A CLI tool for tracking and updating software on macOS. VersionTracker scans your Applications folder, identifies software not managed by the App Store, and suggests Homebrew casks for easy updates. Keep all your Mac software up-to-date with one simple command.

Features:

  • Scans applications not managed by the App Store
  • Identifies Homebrew cask alternatives
  • Recommends installation commands
  • Helps maintain a fully updated macOS system
